FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before booking a free measurement with Eco Screen Sdn Bhd.

Do you provide free measurement?

Yes. Send your location and product interest through WhatsApp, and we will arrange a measurement schedule where available.

Which areas do you cover?

We serve Bukit Mertajam, Batu Kawan, Simpang Ampat, Nibong Tebal, Kulim, Lunas and Penang Island.

What is an invisible roller mosquito screen?

It is a retractable mosquito screen system that can be pulled out when needed and kept neatly when not in use.

Can security screen doors still allow airflow?

Yes. Security screen doors are designed to let natural airflow through while adding a protective barrier.

Can you combine aluminium grille with mosquito screen?

Yes. Aluminium grille and mosquito screen can be planned together depending on the window or door structure.
