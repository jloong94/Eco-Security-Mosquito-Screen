Do you cover Penang and mainland areas?

Yes. We serve Bukit Mertajam, Batu Kawan, Simpang Ampat, Nibong Tebal, Kulim, Lunas and Penang Island.

Can you install for condo balcony doors?

Yes. Roller door, invisible screen and security mesh options can be recommended after checking your opening size and layout.

Which product should I choose?

For insect control, choose mosquito screen or invisible roller screen. For stronger protection, choose security mesh door or security mesh window.

How do I contact Eco Screen?

Click the WhatsApp button on this website or call 019-576 3499.

Can security mesh prevent break-in?

Security mesh adds a strong protective barrier and improves deterrence, but final security performance depends on mesh grade, frame strength, lock type and installation condition.

What is the difference between 304 and 316 mesh?

304 stainless steel is commonly used for general homes. 316 stainless steel has stronger corrosion resistance and is often recommended for seaside or higher-humidity areas.

How long is the warranty?

Security mesh door options can include up to 10 years warranty, while roller screen options can include 3 years warranty depending on product type and final specification.

How long does installation take?

Most standard installations can be completed within a few hours after production is ready. Larger projects may take longer depending on quantity and site condition.

Can install for condo?

Yes. Condo balcony doors, windows and high-rise openings can be measured and fitted with suitable roller screen, sliding net or security mesh options.

Is it suitable for seaside areas?

Yes. For seaside or corrosion-prone locations, ask us about material recommendations such as 316 stainless steel mesh and suitable frame finishing.

Can the screen be removed for cleaning?

Some screen systems are designed for easier cleaning or service access. We will recommend the most suitable option based on the door or window type.
